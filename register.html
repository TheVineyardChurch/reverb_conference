Registration Information

1) The base cost of registration is $139. After February 5th, the price will raise to $155. The cost includes food, lodging, and most activities. Some churches may charge additional money for travel. Speak to your church's leader for more information.

2) You may pay later by choosing "pay later" under payment option. Some churches may elect to have their students pay later and submit a payment directly to their church. Speak to your church's leader for more inforomation.

3) Please be sure to put the STUDENT'S NAME at the beginning of the form (not the parent/guardian name).

4) Sweatshirts are included in the cost of the conference. Additional sizes may not be available if you register late.

5) Feel free to send your student with spending money. We have a cafe with snacks and merchandise available for sale.
