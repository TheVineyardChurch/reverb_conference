Date: Feb 23-25 | Cost: $140 (before Feb 5th) | Where: Lake Williamson Retreat Center

Reverb is the annual Regional Vineyard Conference for students 6th-12th grade. The purpose of this conference is to gather Vineyard Youth Ministries from all around the Midwest (and beyond) to extend the Kingdom, to bring life to our students, and to experience the power and presence of God. We welcome groups of all sizes to join us for this fun, action-packed weekend!

Keynote Speaker

Jeremiah Carlson

Jeremiah with his wife Kara have 3 amazing kids (Emberlin, Lovelin and Valor) and are passionate about seeing people become authentic disciples of Jesus. He was the youth/young adult pastor at the Vancouver Vineyard Church in Vancouver, WA for 10 years as well as the Vineyard Movements NW Youth Task Force Director for 6 years. He is also the lead singer and song writer for the internationally renown worship band "The Neverclaim". He is a champion and preacher for revival over the next generation and has a passion to call out peoples true identity and dreams in Christ.

Worship Leader

Kyle Howard

Kyle's deepest passions are to see people encounter the loving presence of the Father and for people to walk in the fullness of who they were made to be as sons and daughters of the living God. Kyle began leading worship over 10 years ago in his youth ministry at the age of 14. He is featured on worship albums for the National Vineyard and VWorship. Kyle now serves on staff at The Vineyard Church of Central Illinois as Worship Pastor. When he isn't leading worship, Kyle loves to play basketball, golf, and hangout with friends.
