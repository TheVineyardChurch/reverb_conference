WHAT’S THE POINT?

We are built to reverberate the sound of heaven in our everyday life. Our heart is that an entire generation of young people would make, “On earth as it is in Heaven” the cry of their heart. In the schools, in the homes, on the teams, and in the world. Sons and Daughters so fixed on the faithfulness of the Father that everything else in this world fades away. Students from all over the country coming together to to experience an encounter from Heaven that leaves them never the same. Join us this year at Reverb Youth Conference 2019 “The Sound of Heaven.”

WHO IS THIS FOR?

Reverb Youth Conference is designed for students 6th-12th grade. Everything from the fun engaging games to the inspiring messages and the life changing moments in worship. Reverb exist to bring students from across the country into meaningful relationships with new friends and grow their relationship with God.
