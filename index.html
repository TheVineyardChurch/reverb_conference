What is Reverb?

Reverb is the annual Regional Vineyard Conference for students 6th-12th grade. The purpose of this conference is to gather Vineyard Youth Ministries from all around the Midwest (and beyond) to extend the Kingdom, to bring life to our students, and to experience the power and presence of God. We welcome groups of all sizes to join us for this fun, action-packed weekend!

When is Reverb?

Date: Feb 23-25 | Cost: $140 (before Feb 5th) | Where: Lake Williamson Retreat Center
