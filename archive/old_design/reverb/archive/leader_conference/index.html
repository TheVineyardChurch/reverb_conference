Youth Leader Conference 2017

Date: Friday, Oct 6th (7pm)- Saturday, Oct 7th (1pm)

Cost: $30 (includes food on Saturday and lodging)

Where: The Vineyard Church-- Urbana, IL

We want to youth leaders from our region to come and experience a fresh infilling of the Holy Spirit so that they have more to give away! The focus of this conference will be encountering Holy Spirit in a fresh way, building Kingdom Relationships with other youth leaders, and sharing practical insight that will empower youth workers to lead successfully!

You may stay overnight at the church for free or we can send you a list of hotel options in the area.

For more information, contact Leah Wenger at leah.wenger@thevineyardchurch.us
