What To Bring

  • Clothes (don't forget PJs)
  • Coat/gloves/hat
  • Sleeping Bag/Pillow (beds don't have sheets)
  • Swimsuit (one piece/tankinis only)
  • Gym Clothes
  • Shower stuff
  • Towel
  • Any necessary medications
  • Extra money (cafe, book table, etc.)
  • Cell phone/iPods/etc (you're responsible for them)

What Not To Bring

  • Fireworks
  • Cigarettes
  • Alcohol
  • Drugs
  • Or any other bad things
